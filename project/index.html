Our Facility

Specialized Wood Division (3000 sqft):

Within our dedicated wood division, precision and artistry come together to craft exceptional interior elements. Equipped with advanced machinery like Computer Numerical Control (CNC) routers, our carpenters utilize computer-aided design (CAD) software to achieve precise cuts and intricate designs. This cutting-edge technology ensures that every wooden piece is meticulously shaped and tailored to perfection, reflecting our unwavering commitment to quality.

Advanced Metal Division (2500 sqft):

Our specialized metal division employs state-of-the-art machines to create stunning metalwork that enhances the elegance and durability of our interiors. We utilize laser cutting machines and computerized bending equipment, allowing us to achieve intricate patterns and flawless finishes. The use of advanced technology in our metal division ensures the highest level of craftsmanship and attention to detail in each metal element, guaranteeing exceptional quality in every product we deliver.
